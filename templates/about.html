Project Overview

GolfWin is a subscription-based platform where users can enter golf scores, participate in prize draws, and support charities. It focuses on engagement, rewards, and social impact rather than just gameplay.

Core Features

Score Tracking

Manage your last 5 golf scores with smart tracking system.

Monthly Draws

Win rewards through automated draw system.

Charity Integration

Support meaningful causes with every subscription.

Winning Claim & Proof Submission

If a user wins, they must upload proof of their result (screenshot or document). The admin will verify and update the payment status.
